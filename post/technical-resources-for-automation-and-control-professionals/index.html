Technical Resources for Automation and Control Professionals

Jan 30, 2019

Handbooks

  • A Guide to the Automation Body of Knowledge (V. L. Trevathan)
  • Instrument Engineers' Handbook: Process Measurement and Analysis (B. G. Lipták)
  • Instrument Engineers' Handbook: Process Control and Optimization (B. G. Lipták)
  • Instrument Engineers' Handbook: Process Software and Digital Networks (B. G. Lipták)

PID Control

  • Automatic Tuning of Simple Regulators with Specifications on Phase and Amplitude Margins (K. J. Astrom and T. Hagglund)
  • Simple Analytic Rules for Model Reduction and PID Controller Tuning (S. Skogestad)
  • The SIMC Method for Smooth PID Controller Tuning (S. Skogestad and C. Grimholt)

Advanced Process Control

  • Model Predictive Control: Theory, Computation, and Design (D. Q. Mayne, J. B. Rawlings, and M. Diehl)
  • Numerical Optimal Control (Moritz Diehl and Sébastien Gros)
  • Constrained Model Predictive Control: Stability and Optimality (D. Q. Mayne, J. B. Rawlings, C. V. Rao and P. O. M. Scokaert)
  • A Survey of Industrial Model Predictive Control Technology (S. J. Qin and T. A. Badgwell)
  • Feedback Control for Optimal Process Operation (S. Engell)

Numerical Optimization

  • Constrained Optimization and Lagrange Multiplier Methods (D. P. Bertsekas)
  • Parallel and Distributed Computation Numerical Methods (D. P. Bertsekas)
  • Network Optimization: Continuous and Discrete Methods (D. P. Bertsekas)

Industrial Internet of Things

  • Industrial Internet of Things: Challenges, Opportunities, and Directions (E. Sisinni, A. Saifullah, S. Han, U. Jennehag and M. Gidlund)
